Mission Statement

QMIX CONCRETE 2GO CORPORATION focuses on fast and efficient delivery of ready mix concrete product through its on-site portable concrete batching plant. It pursues innovation to provide superior quality products to strengthen the partnership between the company and its customers, and to achieve the highest level of customer satisfaction as well as the company and its employee’s sustainability for them to be able to experience beyond the standard way of living.

Vision Statement

QMIX CONCRETE 2GO CORPORATION envisions itself to be the most dependable and best supplier of ready mix concrete. To be the best means having the highest quality and being the most sought after product, and the most profitable RMC Company in the region.

Why Choose us

At Qmix Corporation, we believe construction success starts with reliable materials. That’s why we go beyond simply supplying concrete and aggregates—we deliver assurance, consistency, and performance that stand the test of time. By choosing Qmix, you’re not just buying products; you’re securing peace of mind for your projects, knowing every batch meets the highest industry standards."
